Cheese Omellete

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk, or as needed
  • Salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded Emmentaler cheese

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs in a bowl; add milk and season with salt and white pepper. Whisk until the egg mixture is foamy, 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Melt butter in a small, nonstick skillet over medium-low heat. Pour in the egg mixture and swirl the skillet so the bottom is evenly covered with egg. Cook until the egg starts to set, about 1 minute.
  3. Lift edges with a spatula and tilt the skillet so uncooked egg can flow towards the bottom of the skillet to set. Repeat until no visible liquid egg remains.
  4. Carefully flip the omelette; cook until warmed through, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  5. Sprinkle Emmentaler cheese down the middle of the omelette; fold in half. Cook until the cheese is melted, about 20 seconds.
  6. Slide the omelette onto a plate.
